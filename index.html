A Bit About Me

I am a classically trained visual artist working in 2D and 3D concept art. I received my Illustration Diploma and Post-Graduate Certificate in Animation, both from Sheridan College in Oakville, Canada.

After working as an illustrator for nearly a decade, I made the move into digital matte painting and concept art for feature films and television, and from there I’ve made my way into concept art, which is my absolute passion.

My artwork and design has won two Royal Canadian Mint awards – International Coin of the Year and Most Innovative Coin.

I grew up in Northern Ireland, just north of Belfast. I currently live in Toronto, Canada.
